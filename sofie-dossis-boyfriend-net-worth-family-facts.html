Published Date: 24 Jun, 2020 Updated date: 23 Apr, 2021 By Daniel Roffer

A self-taught contortionist, hand balancer, aerialist Sofie Dossi has achieved much at the young age. She is often regarded as a spineless girl because of her flexibility.

Sofie Dossi is an American contortionist and Internet celebrity, who has immense followers on her YouTube and Instagram. She was a finalist and the Golden Buzzer recipient on the 11th season of NBC’s reality show, America’s Got Talent.

Sofie Dossi Wiki; Parents, Siblings

Sofie Clarice Dossi was born on 21 June 2001 in Cypress, California, United States. She is the youngest child of Mike Dossi and Abir Dossi. Her father Mike serves as the primary pension advisor at Griffin & Associates Inc, a company based in El Segundo, California. Meanwhile, her mother Abir is a Pilates instructor.

Dossi grew up with an elder brother named Zak Dossi. Zak, 20 is a music producer, known for creating remixes of popular songs on his self-titled Soundcloud and YouTube channel.

Her Career; America’s Got Talent

Sofie got inspired to be a contortionist after watching the performance of the contortionist from ‘Cirque du Soleil’ on the internet. In 2016, she auditioned in the fifth episode of the 11th season of NBC’s reality show America’s Got Talent. Despite being a finalist, she did not receive enough votes and finished in the Bottom 5 of the Top 10 of the season.

Dossi got immense popularity after her appearance in the show. She has appeared on shows including The Ellen DeGeneres Show, The Doctors, Good Mythical Morning, Physics Girl, and The Talent Show’. Besides, she performed as a guest for America’s Got Talent: The Champions in 2019. She has a YouTube channel, which has now amassed more than 5 million subscribers.
