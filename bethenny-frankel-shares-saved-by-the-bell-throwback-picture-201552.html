Bethenny Frankel Shares Saved by the Bell Throwback Picture

Just saw the Saved By The Bell reunion vid from @FallonTonight… So many memories with that cast/crew! #TBT pic.twitter.com/IP3NfpZK1h

— Bethenny Frankel (@Bethenny) February 5, 2015

Thursday, Feb. 5, 2015 has officially become Saved by the Bell throwback day. After a majority of the cast from the ‘90s sitcom reunited on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon on Wednesday, Feb. 4, the Internet blew up with throwback tributes to the Bayside High show. Even Skinnygirl founder and Real Housewives of New York City star Bethenny Frankel got in on the fun.

“Just saw the Saved By The Bell reunion vid from @FallonTonight… So many memories with that cast/crew! #TBT,” the entrepreneur shared on Twitter on Thursday, along with an amazing throwback photo from when she was a production assistant.

In the snap, the mom to Bryn, 4, rocks a seriously ‘90s oversized sweatshirt and smiles alongside Dustin Diamond, who played Screech.

The now hugely successful businesswoman and re-upped reality star first revealed she worked as a PA during a 2012 interview with Mario Lopez on her since-cancelled talk show.

"When I first moved to L.A., all I wanted to do was be an actress," she admitted to her audience before bringing out Lopez, who played hunk Slater. "I wanted to be one of those actors or celebrities. They looked like they were having so much fun…And now, my first guest today is one of the people that was on that show [I worked on], which is so mind-blowing that now he's my guest. So, bring out Mario Lopez!"

And Lopez more than remembered Frankel! 

"It's funny because you show that picture from Saved by the Bell and I'm like, 'I totally remember that!'" he admitted. “Bethenny was so sweet and so cool with everybody, but I had a little crush on her!”
