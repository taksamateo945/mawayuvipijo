First Muppets Trailer for ABC Is All Kinds of Awesome: Watch

Is it fall yet? ABC debuted the first trailer for its highly anticipated new primetime Muppets series on Tuesday, May 12, and it's everything you could want — and more!

As the trailer explains, the new Muppets show is a documentary-style comedy — a la Modern Family or The Office — about Kermit and Co.'s personal lives. "Sort of an adult Muppets show," Kermit explains to his costars.

The series, which will air on Tuesdays at 8 p.m. this fall, stars all your favorite Jim Henson characters: Kermit, Gonzo, Fozzie, Animal, Beaker, and "some incredibly obscure Muppets that nobody remembers."
