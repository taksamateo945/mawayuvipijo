Hazel Krasinski: All About Emily Blunt and Actor John Krasinskis First Child

Hazel Krasinski entered the world in 2014 at St. Elizabeth’s Medical Center in Boston, Massachusetts, located in the United States of America.

She is the offspring of actor John Krasinski and actress Emily Blunt.

Upon her birth, her father, John Krasinski, took to Twitter to proudly announce her arrival. He posted, “Wanted to let the news out directly.

Emily and I are so incredibly happy to welcome our daughter Hazel into the world today! Happy bday!”

Hazel became the firstborn child of actor John Krasinski and actress Emily Blunt, and she holds dual nationality – American by birth and British through her mother’s heritage.

Krasinski also has a younger sister born in 2016.

Emily Blunt, their mother, has expressed her hope that both Hazel and her sister can remain blissfully unaware of their parents’ celebrity status for as long as possible.

The Krasinski’s family

The spotlight is no stranger to this family. John Krasinski and Emily Blunt, two of Hollywood’s most adored celebrities, form a beloved couple.

They crossed paths in 2008, introduced by a mutual friend, and their love story unfolded rapidly.

Just a year later, they became engaged and sealed their commitment in a beautiful ceremony in Lake Como, Italy, in 2010.

John Krasinski, renowned for his portrayal of Jim Halpert on the hit sitcom “The Office,” has left an indelible mark in both television and film.

Emily Blunt has earned recognition for her performances in films such as “The Devil Wears Prada,” and “A Quiet Place,”.

The couple has also ventured into production, collaborating on projects like “A Quiet Place” and “A Quiet Place Part II.”

Adding to the family’s charm, Hazel has a younger sister named Violet Krasinski.

Hazel Krasinski Net Worth

Hazel Krasinski, a young student, is currently immersed in her elementary education.

Consequently, she relies on her parents for support and hasn’t started earning income or amassing assets for assessment.

Her parents, John Krasinski and Emily Blunt, boast a combined net worth of $80 million.

They have achieved remarkable success in their acting careers and have collaborated in producing several films.
