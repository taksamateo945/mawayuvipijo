Counting On Fans Are Suspicious Of Jessa Seewald

Counting On alum Jessa Seewald went very quiet on social media but all of a sudden, she started posting nine to the dozen, as if her life depended on it.

So, that makes TLC fans very suspicious. Many of them believe that Jim Bob Duggar will get the kids to divert attention away from things he doesn’t want them to know.

Is that what went down in the last day or so?

Counting On Alum Jessa Seewald Announced Her Pregnancy

Happy that she fell pregnant and expects a fifth child, Jesse mentioned it very briefly in a video on her YouTube. Then, she went on and revealed a lot about their getaway.

It was the first one ever. Of course, the kids were seen as well. However, people wonder why she chose now to tell her followers about the new baby.

After all, it’s not like it’s something that seldom happens in the Duggar family.

Some Counting On fans suspect it might be because her much more popular sister, Jill Dillard was on the cusp of launching her new book called Counting The Cost.

Of course, it seemed that Jim Bob and Ben Seewald were not overly thrilled about Jill in Amazon’s docuseries.

She let a lot of cats out of the bag when she opened up on Shiny Happy People: Duggar Family Secrets.

So, it’s unlikely that they celebrate a Tell-All, memoir that makes the family look bad. Already, some alarming content leaked.

 

Counting On – Is Jessa Posting For Distraction From Jill’s Book?

Redditors seem convinced that Jill’s sister is posting in order to take the focus off Jill and her book.

There’s been a lot of interest in it, and some people believe that Jim Bob might have told Jessa Seewald to post more. On Reddit, u/HiiViolet noted; “In case you were keeping track…That would be 16, yes sixteen posts Jessa made Sunday night as a ninth inning distraction from Jill’s book release.”
