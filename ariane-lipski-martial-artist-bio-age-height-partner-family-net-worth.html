Ariane Lipski Biography

Ariane Lipski also known as”Queen of Violence” is a Brazilian professional mixed martial artist who is currently competing in the Flyweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). Moreover, she is the former champion of the Konfrontacja Sztuk Walki match. In March 2023, Lipski fought with JJ Aldrich at UFC Fight Night: Yan vs. Dvalishvili and emerged as the winner of the match. Additionally, she was born and brought up in Brazil, where she attended a Muay Thai training center.

Later, she furthered her training as a Brazilian Muay Thai champion before transitioning to MMA. At the age of 18, she met her current coach, Renato Rasta, and began to see MMA as a viable career path. Moreover, Ariane mostly fought in Brazil and Poland under the Konfrontacja Sztuk Walki promotion. She made her debut appearance at KSW Women’s Flyweight Championship where she fought with Diana Belbita and emerged as the winner in the first round.

Following her success in her first match, she fought with several fighters like Mariana Morais and Silvana Gómez Juárez and won those matches. Thereafter, she was signed with UFC and made her first debut with the team against Joanne Calderwood. In 2019, she also appeared in her second match at UFC Fight Night: Moicano vs. Korean Zombie in which she was defeated. After that, she was scheduled to face Priscila Cachoeira at UFC on ESPN however, Priscila tested positive for diuretic and the match was postponed.
