Download Flashnote Portable for Windows - Free

How often do you need to take simple, quick notes? An email address, a phone number, maybe something important you need to remember or something interesting you saw online... This is where Flashnote comes in handy. This tiny note-taking app is lighter, faster, and simpler to use than Windows Notepad itself. Whenever you need to jot something down, simply click the program's icon on the system tray - or hit the customizable keyboard shortcut - and Flashnote will appear on the screen, ready to store whatever you write.

Notes in Flashnote are organized in a tree-like structure, which makes them easier to sort and browse. The program saves its content automatically (even between sessions) and also lets you export all your notes to TXT format.

Flashnote has some basic formatting features that enable you to change the background and text color. However, there's no option to insert bold or italics, and the program doesn't recognize email addresses or URLs automatically either.

Flashnote helps you store and manage those quick notes we all need to take from time to time with no hassle.
