FOXY BROWN'S DAUGHTER MAKES A RARE APPEARANCE IN NEW PHOTO

Foxy Brown’s daughter, C’yani, is growing up before our eyes!

On Saturday, Brown’s former groupmate, Az, shared a rare photo of the six-year-old. In the snap, C’yani is seated next to him, smiling at the camera.

AZ captioned,”Went to check up on @foxybrown wanted to let her know the world waiting on her and …. and while doing that guess who I ran into?? Her little twin!! The Beautiful Baby C A young fox Boogie!!”

C’yani recenty celebrated her 6th birthday, and Foxy Brown couldn’t help but shower her with love on Instagram. The rapper shared a heartfelt message,saying, “HAPPY 6TH BIRTHDAY TO THE ONE! ONLY ONE I LIVE FOR AND DIE FOR! MY G BABY C! LOVE YOU MORE THAN MYSELF, NANA!”

Foxy Brown has been candid about the challenges she faced during C’yani’s birth. The rapper, whose real name is Inga Marchand, shared the emotional journey on Instagram, recalling the miraculous delivery of her “gorgeous, greatest hit”.

“It was exactly 12:29 a.m,” Brown told her fans. “The excruciating pain in my 8-month pregnant belly said I was losing her! I’d lost an obscene amount of blood for weeks (diagnosed with Placenta Previa). My baby was dying in me. My holy spirit, though. Thank you, Jesus! I delivered the most gorgeous baby girl, C’Yani, by cesarean just a few hours later. … My incredibly gorgeous, greatest hit. … Obsessively in love.”

As for the gossip surrounding her baby’s father, rumored to be rapper Spragga Benz, Foxy Brown has affirmed her role as a single parent. She recently stated, “I do it solo. Thank you, Lord.”.

In 2015, Foxy Brown revealed that Spragga Benz was the only man to ever own her heart. They met in Trinidad 16 years ago, experiencing love at first sight. Foxy referred to him as her soulmate. However, the pair has since parted ways
