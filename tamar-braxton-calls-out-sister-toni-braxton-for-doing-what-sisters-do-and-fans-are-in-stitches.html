Tamar Braxton Calls Out Sister Toni Braxton for Doing What Sisters Do and Fans Are in Stitches

Tamar Braxton has a bone to pick with her sister Toni Braxton. The newly boo’d up singer called out her older sister on Instagram Wednesday, Aug. 1 after she caught the “Sex and Cigarettes” singer wearing a set of her pajamas.

“She’s so BUSTED in MY pajamas that I have been looking for 🙄 @tonibraxton #pjthief,” Tamar wrote in the caption of a dolled up Toni in her sleepwear.

Fans quickly began chiming in on the typical sibling incident of taking one another’s clothes without the other’s permission.

“😂😂❤Sister Love 😍.”

“@tamarbraxton definitely something I would have [done]. Lol.”

“@theegoogoo facts, my sisters & I do it to each other every time.”

“@tamarbraxton 🤣🤣🤣🤣🤣🤣🤣 she hit you with the barter system.”

“😂😂😂😂😂!! My son used to do this with his brother’s clothes… Take pictures with them on…”

However, the youngest Braxton sister didn’t need to fret for long. Toni posted on Instagram a day later that she had returned  Tamar’s pink jammies, trading them in for a robe as she waited on set on her new Christmas movie she’s filming for Lifetime.

“I gave @tamarbraxton her pajamas back, so now I’m on set in my robe 💃🏽,” she wrote in the caption of a photo from the Vancouver shooting location.

The Braxton siblings — which includes sisters  Towanda, Traci and Trina — are no strangers to squabbling on public platforms. The vocalists have had their fair share of that on their WEtv reality series, “Braxton Family Values.”

Still, the sisters have had some bonding during the latest season of the show.

“[This season] made me and my sisters a little bit closer,” Traci told The Grio in March. “Closer than what we normally are. We share each other’s pain and hurt. You may not see it on the air, but off the air, we would try to hug on each other besides ignoring each other. And right now you know, everybody don’t wanna hug.”
