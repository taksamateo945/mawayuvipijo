Natasha Thahane Celebrates Hitting 4 Million Instagram Followers

- Natasha Thahane is celebrating a social media milestone after she hit four million followers on Instagram

- The star posted to the platform in order to commemorate the moment and shared a video compilation of her followers dancing

- Natasha expressed gratitude to her fans and thanked the supporters who have been with her along the way

Natasha Thahane has ended the first month of 2021 with a win by hitting a social media milestone.

The actress and businesswoman has managed to amass a whopping four million followers on her Instagram account.

Natasha posted a sweet message and video to her account to commemorate the moment. The footage was a compilation of some of her fans celebrating and dancing.

The star thanked all her fans for getting her to the large following statistic and expressed gratitude to the ones who have been along for the ride with her.

Natasha captioned her post:

"4 MILLION Put together a video of some of my followers dancing, this is our mood!! Thank you for always supporting my work. To many more!"
