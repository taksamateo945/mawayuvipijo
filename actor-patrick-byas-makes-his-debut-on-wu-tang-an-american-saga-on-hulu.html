Actor Patrick Byas Makes His Debut on Wu-Tang: An American Saga on Hulu

Actor Patrick Byas has just made his debut on Hulu Show Wu-Tang: An American Saga. Patrick portrayed the character of a God. Social media was in uproar when his episode launched. Patrick has worked on many others including “The First Purge”, “Blue Bloods”, “Chicago PD” “Law & Order SVU”, and “Billions”.

The show tracks the Wu Tang Clan’s formation, a vision of Bobby Diggs, who strives to unite a dozen young, black men that are torn between music and crime but eventually rise to become the unlikeliest of American success stories.

Patrick is currently on an exciting media tour, and he just finished up an interview on SiriusXM with personality Alex Di Trolio.

The two rising stars reflected on how they met, discussed new roles and opportunities, encouragement, motivating others, and even had some time for a few laughs in between.

We also see that Patrick is currently working on or has just finished a movie called “The Deal”. It is said on “The Deal’s” IMDB page that they are in post production and we’re looking forward to checking that out.
