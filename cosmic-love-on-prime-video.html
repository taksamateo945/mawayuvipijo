&quot;Cosmic Love&quot; On Prime Video

There’s a unique new social experiment coming to Prime Video and BOSSIP recently chatted with the astrologers behind it.

“Cosmic Love” will debut on August 12 on the streaming service and it follows four individuals who attempt to find their perfect spouse via astrological matchmaking.

Source: Cosmic Love / Prime Video

Each individual represents one of the four elements of astrology (Air, Fire, Water, Earth) and fireworks ensue when they live together at a retreat and meet 16 singles vying for their affection.

Source: Cosmic Love / Prime Video

Source: Cosmic Love / Prime Video

In an interesting twist to the reality show, the retreat is run by a mystical guide called the Astro Chamber. Voiced by Cree Summer, the Astro Chamber is led by the show’s astrologers; best-selling authors Ophira and Tali Edut, also known as the AstroTwins who encourage the group to trust the stars to find their person.

The elements mingle, match, date, and eliminate singles before eventually making the biggest decision of their lives… will they marry their match, based solely on their astrology?

Source: Cosmic Love / Prime Video

“Cosmic Love” premieres all 10 episodes on Prime Video in more than 240 countries and territories worldwide on Friday, August 12.

The AstroTwins Weigh In On Celebrity Matches With BOSSIP

The AstroTwins recently chatted with BOSSIP’s Managing Editor Dani Canada about not only the one-of-a-kind social experiment but about celebrity couples. Were things ever REALLY in the stars for Michael B. Jordan and Lori Harvey who broke up in June? Michael is of course an air sign; an Aquarius and Lori is an earth sign; a Capricorn.

According to the AstroTwins, mixing earth with air can come with complications.

“Any two signs can make it work but air and earth can be the harder two to bring together because they operate at such different speeds, the earth is down here trying to hold things, air needs to circulate and move around. Unless they’re comfortable really being those roles, the earth sign can be frustrated always trying to pull the air sign back.”

In addition to giving their insight on Michael and Lori, the twins also spoke on rekindled couple Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck who recently tied the knot. Are the two Leos a perfect match for each other? It sure seems that way!

Meet the ‘Cosmic Love’ Cast – Elements

Maria Rodriguez | Capricorn (@xmariasbeautyparty) – Earth Element

Hardworking Capricorn Maria Rodriguez, 28, is the Earth Element on Cosmic Love. Raised by powerful women in New York City, beauty #boss Maria is a professional makeup artist who owns several successful businesses, including her own brand, M Beauty Party. Fiercely loyal to family and her Dominican and Puerto Rican roots, this savvy Sea Goat is not willing to settle. True to her zodiac sign’s take-charge style, Maria plans everything in her life to perfection. But can she surrender control to make room for a powerful plus-one?

Phoebe Davis | Leo (@flexingphoebs) – Fire Element

It’s getting hot in here! Double Leo entrepreneur, model and fitness competitor Phoebe Davis, 28, is the consummate cosmic Lioness whose roar has scared off more than a couple of players. Now, as the Fire Element on Cosmic Love, this queen is ready to find her “mane” man to claim the adjacent throne. A native of Spokane, Washington, Phoebe lives in San Diego, where she runs a marketing business and a personal brand, “Flexing Phoebs,” inspiring women worldwide to love their bodies, overcome adversity, and embrace personal growth. Will Phoebe fall into the fire-sign trap of burning through her matches…or will she find someone who can keep the blazes lit for the long haul?

Connor Shennan | Gemini (@shennanconnor) – Air Element

Double the trouble or twice the fun? Gemini Connor Shennan, 27, represents the Air Element on Cosmic Love. True to his zodiac sign’s curious nature, Connor needs to weigh every option before making a (gulp) decision. Will this wildland firefighter find his “twin flame” on Cosmic Love? Or will the winds of change send his attention in a new direction? A lover of music, sports and the great outdoors, Connor grew up in a close-knit Michigan family and currently lives in Phoenix.

Noel Allen | Pisces (@superfitnoel) – Water Element

Pisces Noel Allen, 31, is a personal trainer and nutrition coach tapped to be the Water Element on Cosmic Love.  True to his artistic and caring zodiac sign, Noel is a romantic renaissance man who loves cooking, anime, working out, and supporting his clients as they transform their bodies and lives. Raised by a Cuban-American mom in New Jersey, Noel lives in Tampa, and hopes to create his own family. But here’s the (Fish) hook: Noel won’t water down his desire for both freedom and security, one of the many paradoxes of Pisces men that drive women crazy…and makes them fall crazy in love.
