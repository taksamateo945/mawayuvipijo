Rebel Wilson shows off weight loss and announces directorial debut for 2022

Rebel Wilson has lost more than 30 kilograms as part of her ‘year of health’.

And the Australian actress looked nothing short of sensational in a chic red jumpsuit, in on-set photos shared to her Instagram Stories on Saturday.

The 41-year-old Pitch Perfect star also used the opportunity to announce that her directorial debut is slated for 2022. 

Red hot! Rebel Wilson (pictured), 41, showed off her trim waist and slimmed-down figure in a chic red jumpsuit in an Instagram Stories post on Saturday, as she announces her directorial debut is slated for 2022

Rebel accentuated her trim waist and slimmed-down figure in the vibrant jumpsuit, which she teamed with a puffy sleeved white blouse, worn underneath.

She boosted her height with coordinating red pointy-toe heels, and her beauty look included structured blonde retro curls, striking eye makeup and a glossy nude lip.

Also on her Instagram Stories, Rebel announced that her directorial debut is slated for 2022, with auditions in late 2021.

The Bridesmaids star accompanied the text with the image of four pop stars and the caption ‘Girl Group’.

In December 2019, Deadline reported that Rebel wrote a script for a movie based on K-pop, titled Seoul Girls, with motion picture group Lionsgate landing the rights. 

The film will reportedly follow ‘a Korean American high school girl and her friends, who enter a competition to be the opening act for a popular K-pop boy band.

‘The Seoul Girls find their voices on the world’s biggest stage,’ Deadline revealed.

Working hard: Rebel made 2020 her ‘year of health’ and worked hard to overhaul her diet and fitness habits, losing more than 30 kilograms

Meanwhile over the past year, Rebel has completely overhauled her lifestyle and lost more than 30 kilograms in the process. 

According to People magazine, Rebel’s remarkable transformation is partially down to following the Mayr Method diet plan.

‘It’s an approach that eliminates food intolerances, reduces sugar, encourages eating whole foods slowly, boosts the immune system and reduces inflammation,’ a source said.

‘She exercises with a personal trainer up to six times a week, goes on walks and is trying to up her protein intake nutritionally.’
