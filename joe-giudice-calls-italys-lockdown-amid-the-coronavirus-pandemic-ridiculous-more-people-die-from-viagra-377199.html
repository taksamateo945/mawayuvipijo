Joe Giudice Calls Italys Lockdown Amid The Coronavirus Pandemic Ridiculous More Peop

Italy is now on lockdown amid the whole Coronavirus crisis and Joe Giudice, who has been living there while waiting for a final decision in his deportation case, made it very clear he does not agree! As far as the reality TV star is concerned, the measures are too extreme since the virus is really not that dangerous at all!

Joe shared his opinion on the situation on video while showing how his Salerno neighborhood looked like a complete ‘ghost town’ because of the quarantine.

Teresa Giudice’s estranged husband mocked the fears as well in the clip he posted on his Instagram page by pointing out that even Viagra pills cause more deaths than the Covid19 strand of the Coronavirus!

Joe filmed the empty streets, sidewalk and the buildings in his neighborhood and called the lockdown ‘ridiculous.’

Obviously frustrated, Joe went on to explain that ‘More people die from Viagra (heart attacks and drug overdose) everyday than this Coronavirus. People here are so frantic because government’s ridiculous with 🔒down !!!! Sorry Lockdown not for me Never again !!!!! Stay safe 🌎 eat healthy, exercise, no shaking and 🧼 👏!’
