Robbie Brookside (Wrestler) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Robert Edward Brooks, known by his stage name Robbie Brookside, is a retired British professional wrestler. He is currently under contract with WWE, where he works as a trainer / producer for the NXT brand. He has toured around the world during his career, wrestling in the United States, Japan, Germany and Mexico. He is Steve Regal's regular partner in the UK and competed at New Japan Pro Wrestling's annual Super J tournament in 1997, where he won against Chris Jericho.
